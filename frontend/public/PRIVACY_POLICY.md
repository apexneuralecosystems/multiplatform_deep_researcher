## 1. Personal Data We Collect

### 1.1 Data Provided by You

When you use our Platform, you may voluntarily provide:

- Full name, email address, phone number, and company name
- Login or account registration details
- Billing and payment information (if you purchase paid plans)
- Files, text, documents, datasets, media or any content uploaded by you ("User Content")

### 1.2 Data Collected Automatically

We automatically collect:

- IP address, device and browser type, operating system
- Usage metrics such as pages visited, actions performed, features used, session duration
- Cookies, pixel tags, web beacons and similar tracking technologies (See Section 10)

### 1.3 Data Generated by Our AI Services

- Inputs submitted to our AI systems
- Outputs generated by the Platform in response to your inputs
- Conversation history, customization preferences, and feedback

### 1.4 Definition of Personal Data

"Personal data" means any data relating to an identifiable individual. It does not include anonymized or aggregated data that cannot identify you.

### 1.5 Data Specific to Multiplatform Deep Researcher Services

In addition to the data categories described above, when you use our Multiplatform Deep Researcher services, we collect and process:

**Research Inputs:**
- Research queries, topics, and search terms you submit
- Platform preferences and research parameters you configure
- Research session identifiers and timestamps

**Research Outputs:**
- Generated research reports and synthesized findings
- Extracted content from web sources and social media platforms
- URLs and links collected during research sessions
- Platform-specific data (Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, X, web) gathered during research

**Technical Data:**
- WebSocket connection information and session data
- Real-time research progress and status updates
- API request logs and response data
- Third-party data provider usage metrics (e.g., Bright Data MCP usage)

**Important Note:** Our research services collect publicly available information from web sources and social media platforms. We do not access private accounts, protected content, or non-public information. All data collection is limited to publicly accessible content only.

## 7. Sharing & Disclosure of Personal Data

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with:

- Cloud hosting providers and data processors (with equivalent or higher protection obligations)
- Payment processors and fraud prevention partners
- Professional advisors, attorneys, auditors, or insurers where necessary
- Governmental or regulatory authorities if legally required
- Third parties in case of merger, acquisition, or business transfer (data will continue under this Policy)

**Third-Party Data Providers for Research Services:**

To provide our Multiplatform Deep Researcher services, we share research queries and parameters with third-party data providers and web scraping services, including:

- **Bright Data MCP**: We share research queries and search parameters with Bright Data to enable web scraping and data collection from various platforms. Bright Data processes these queries to collect publicly available web content on our behalf.
- **OpenRouter**: We share research queries with OpenRouter to access AI models (GPT-4o, Gemini, o3-mini) for search, analysis, and report generation.

These third-party providers process your research queries to deliver the requested services. They do not receive your personal account information unless necessary for service delivery. All third-party processors act only under our instructions and legal agreements.

We may also share anonymized or aggregated data that does not identify individuals for research, analytics, business insights, or product improvement purposes.

Your data is primarily stored and processed in India. If transferred outside India, we ensure adequate safeguards consistent with DPDP Act standards and applicable international requirements.

## 9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard organizational and technical controls, including:

- Encryption at rest (AES-256 where applicable)
- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Access control and authentication procedures
- Regular vulnerability assessment and monitoring

**Security Measures for Research Data:**

- **WebSocket Security**: All real-time research updates are transmitted over encrypted WebSocket connections (WSS)
- **API Security**: Research queries and data are transmitted using secure HTTPS/TLS protocols
- **Third-Party Data Providers**: We work with reputable data providers (e.g., Bright Data) that implement their own security measures for web scraping and data collection
- **Session Management**: Research sessions are isolated and managed securely with unique session identifiers
- **Data Isolation**: Research data is stored separately from account data and is accessible only to authorized users
- **Access Logging**: We maintain logs of research activities for security monitoring and audit purposes

However, no electronic transmission or storage method is 100% secure. While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, you acknowledge that research data transmitted over the internet or stored on our systems may be subject to unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.
